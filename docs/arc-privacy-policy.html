Privacy Policy

ARC — Agent Remote Control

Last updated: June 23, 2026

ARC is a self-hosted personal productivity tool that lets you run and manage AI agents remotely. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

When you sign in via Facebook (or another OAuth provider), ARC stores:

  • Your name and email address as provided by the OAuth provider
  • A provider-specific user ID to link your account
  • Session tokens for authentication
  • Preferences and configuration you set within ARC

ARC does not collect payment information, location data, or browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Store your ARC preferences and agent configurations
  • Associate tasks and scheduled jobs with your account

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

ARC is self-hosted. All data is stored on your own server in a local SQLite database. Anthropic, Meta, or any other OAuth provider have no access to data stored within ARC.

4. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing mjshuff23@gmail.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request. We will process requests within 30 days.

For Facebook-specific deletion instructions, see the Facebook Data Deletion page.

5. Third-Party OAuth Providers

ARC supports sign-in via Google, Facebook, Microsoft, GitHub, LinkedIn, and X/Twitter. When you use one of these providers, their own privacy policies also apply to the authentication step. ARC only receives the profile data (name, email, user ID) that you authorize during sign-in.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to mjshuff23@gmail.com.

ARC is a personal project by Michael Shuff. This policy applies to the ARC application only.
